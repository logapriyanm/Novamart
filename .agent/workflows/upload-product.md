---
description: How to upload a product to NovaMart as a Manufacturer
---

Follow these steps to upload a new product to the NovaMart platform:

1.  **Login to Manufacturer Portal**: 
    - Ensure you are logged in as a **Manufacturer**.
    - If you are not registered, go to `/auth/register?role=MANUFACTURER`.

2.  **Navigate to Product Master**:
    - Click on "Product Master" or "Inventory" in your dashboard navigation.
    - Path: `/manufacturer/products`.

3.  **Initiate Product Addition**:
    - Click the **"Add New Product"** button located at the top-right of the Product Master page.

4.  **Complete the Multi-Step Form**:
    - **Step 1: General Info**: Enter the Product Name, Category (select "Home Appliances"), and a detailed Description.
    - **Step 2: Specifications**: Add technical specs like Power Rating, Energy Efficiency (Star rating), Dimensions, and Warranty.
    - **Step 3: Pricing**: Enter the Base Price (Manufacturer's selling price) and Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ).
    - **Step 4: Media**: Upload high-quality product images. At least one image is required for the catalog.
    - **Step 5: Compliance**: Verify that the product meets NovaMart governance standards.
    - **Step 6: Review**: Review all entered data for accuracy.

5.  **Submit for Approval**:
    - Click **"Submit for Approval"**.
    - If your account is **Verified**, the product will go live instantly.
    - If not, it will enter the **"Pending Approval"** state and will be audited by the Admin team.

6.  **Track Status**:
    - You can monitor the status (Live, Pending, Rejected) in your **Product Master** dashboard.
